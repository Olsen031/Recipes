Apple Pie

Ingredients Directions
3 pounds mixed apples(ex: Granny Smith, Gala, and McIntosh) Peel and core the apples; slice 1/4 inch thick.
2/3 cup granulated sugar Toss with the granulated sugar and lemon juice.
2 tablespoons fresh lemon juice Heat 4 tablespoons butter in a large skillet over medium-high heat.
6 tablespoons unsalted butter Add the apples and cook, stirring, until tender, about 10 minutes.
1 tablespoons all-purpose flour, pluse more for dusting Stir in the flour, cinnamon and salt; cook until thickened, 1 more minute. Let cool.
3/4 teaspoon ground cinnamon Roll out 1 disk of dough into a 12-inch round on a floured surface; ease into a 9-inch pie plate.
1/8 teaspoon salt Add the apples, mounding them slightly in the center, and dot with the remaining 2 tablespoons butter.
2 disks dough for Basic Crust Roll out the remaining dough into a 12-inch round. Lay it over the filling; press the edges of the crusts together, then fold the overhang under itself and crimp to seal.
1 large egg, beaten Brush with the beaten egg and sprinkle with coarse sugar. Cut a few slits in the top crust. Chill 1 hour.
Coarse sugar, for sprinkling Place a baking sheet on the lowest oven rack and preheat to 425 degrees F for 30 minutes.
Place the pie on the hot baking sheet; lower the temperature to 375 degrees F.
Bake until golden, 1 hour to 1 hour, 10 minutes. (Cover the edges with foil if they brown too quickly.) Cool on a rack.

Pumpkin Pie

Ingredients Directions
1 disk dough for Cream Cheese Crust Roll out the dough into a 12-inch round on a lightly floured surface.
All-purpose flour, for dusting Ease into a 9-inch pie plate; fold the overhang under itself and crimp.
1 15-ounce can pure pumpkin Chill 30 minutes. Preheat the oven to 350 degrees F.
1 cup evaporated milk Prick the bottom of the crust with a fork.
2/3 cup sugar Line with foil, fill with dried beans and bake until the edges are golden, about 20 minutes.
2 large eggs Remove the beans and foil and bake 10 more minutes. Cool on a rack.
1 teaspoon ground cinnamon Whisk the pumpkin, evaporated milk, sugar, eggs and spices in a bowl; pour into the crust.
1/2 teaspoon ground ginger Bake until the center is just barely set, about 1 hour. (Cover the edges with foil if they brown too quickly.)
1/4 teaspoon freshly grated nutmeg Cool on a rack.

Pecan Pie

Ingredients Directions
1 disk dough for Basic Crust Roll out the dough into a 12-inch round on a lightly floured surface.
All-purpose flour, for dusting Ease into a 9-inch pie plate; fold the overhang under itself and crimp. Chill 30 minutes.
6 tablespoons butter, melted Place a baking sheet on the lowest oven rack and preheat to 425 degrees F for 30 minutes.
2 large eggs Meanwhile, make the filling: Whisk all of the remaining ingredients except the pecans in a bowl.
3/4 cup packed light brown sugar Spread the pecans in the crust and pour in the filling.
3/4 cup light corn syrup Place the pie on the hot baking sheet and lower the oven temperature to 325 degrees F.
1 teaspoon fresh lemon juice Bake until the crust is golden and the filling is set, 45 to 55 minutes. (Cover the edges with foil if they brown too quickly.)
2 teaspoons vanilla extract Cool on a rack.
1/4 teaspoon salt
2 1/4 cups pecan halves, toasted

Chocolate Pie

Ingredients Directions
2 cups milk Heat the milk in a large saucepan until hot but not boiling.
1/2 cup sugar Whisk the sugar, cornstarch and salt in a large bowl, then whisk in the egg yolks, coffee and vanilla.
1/4 cup cornstarch Whisk half of the hot milk into the egg mixture until smooth, then gradually whisk the egg mixture into the pan with the remaining milk.
1/2 teaspoon salt Cook over medium heat, whisking constantly, until the mixture boils and thickens, 3 to 5 minutes.
4 large egg yolks Remove from the heat and whisk in the chocolate until melted.
2 tablespoons brewed coffee, cooled Transfer to a bowl and cool slightly, stirring a few times to prevent a skin from forming.
1/2 teaspoon vanilla extract Pour the filling into the crust; press plastic wrap directly onto the surface and chill until set, at least 4 hours.
4 ounces semisweet chocolate, chopped Top with whipped cream and shaved chocolate.
1 Chocolate Crumb Crust
Whipped cream and shaved chocolate, for topping

Coconut Cream Pie

Ingredients Directions
1/2 (15-ounce) package refrigerated piecrusts Fit 1 piecrust into a 9-inch pie plate according to package directions; fold edges under, and crimp.
1/2 cup sugar Prick bottom and sides of piecrust with a fork. Bake according to package directions for a one-crust pie.
1/4 cup cornstarch Combine 1/2 cup sugar and cornstarch in a heavy saucepan.
2 cups half-and-half Whisk together half-and-half and egg yolks.
4 egg yolks Gradually whisk egg mixture into sugar mixture; bring to a boil over medium heat, whisking constantly. Boil 1 minute; remove from heat.
3 tablespoons butter Stir in butter, 1 cup coconut, and 1 teaspoon vanilla.
1 cup sweetened flaked coconut Cover with plastic wrap, placing plastic wrap directly on filling in pan; let stand 30 minutes.
2 1/2 teaspoons vanilla extract, divided Spoon custard mixture into prepared crust, cover and chill 30 minutes or until set.
2 cups whipping cream Beat whipping cream at high speed with an electric mixer until foamy; gradually add 1/3 cup sugar and remaining 1 1/2 teaspoons vanilla, beating until soft peaks form.
1/3 cup sugar Spread or pipe whipped cream over pie filling.
Garnish: toasted coconut Garnish, if desired.
